GOD AND QUANTUM ROCKET 神与量子火箭（2021）

→ 简介|Synopsis
电影贯穿于一个列车上的男子对于童年的回忆中，同时穿插成年后的自己对记忆的思考。
拍摄自故乡中国和国外各种影像——几乎都是没有人的镜头，但人的身影反复被暗示——被交叉剪辑重组，以此重现童年时的经历。
影片重现了一个儿童心中主观的、不真实的外界世界，与他内心世界的成长改变相交错。
作者用影像寄托着他对记忆、现实和成长的思考。

The movie runs through a man on a train's childhood memories,
and at the same time intersperses his own thinking about memories as an adult.
Various images taken from their hometowns of China and abroad-almost all shots of no one,
but human figures are repeatedly implied-were cross-edited and reorganized to recreate the experience of childhood.
The film reproduces a child's subjective and unreal outside world,
which is intertwined with the growth and change of his inner world.
The author uses images to entrust his thoughts on memory, reality and growth.
