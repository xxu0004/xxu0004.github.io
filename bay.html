BAY AREA SYSTEM 湾区系统（2018）

 

 

 

→ 项目选址|Location

→ 项目说明|Description

城市、自然、土地、海洋、生产、污染。
多种问题并不意味着无可救药，
而是意味着系统有待更新。
City, nature, land, ocean, production, pollution.
Multiple problems don’t mean hopeless,
It means that the system needs to be updated.

城区的扩张导致栖息地的破坏；
水污染导致生态平衡被打破；
物种的减少导致生产效率降低；
寻求工业对生产的拯救导致水污染……
所有的问题都是相互关联的。
The expansion of the urban area leads to the destruction of the habitat;
Water pollution causes the ecological balance to be broken;
The reduction of species leads to lower production efficiency;
Seeking industrial rescue of production leads to water pollution...
All issues are interrelated.

重建系统的链条。
让食物链上的每一种生物拥有捕食与被捕食的平衡，
从浮游生物直至城市居民的餐桌。
Rebuild the chain of the system.
Let every creature in the food chain have a balance between predation and predation,
From plankton to the dining table of city dwellers.

让每一种生物拥有其栖息地。
自山地、农田、湿地，
到潮间带、海湾、海洋。
也让每一种土地成为人类的生产之所。
自住区到渔场、牡蛎滩、潮汐站。
Let every creature have its habitat.
From mountain, farmland, wetland,
To intertidal zone, open water, deep sea.
Also let every kind of land become a place of human production.
From residential area to the fishing farm, oyster beach, and tide stations

重新规划的用地，
重新接合的系统，
重新平衡的泉州湾。
Re-planned land,
Rejoined system,
Rebalanced Quanzhou Bay.
